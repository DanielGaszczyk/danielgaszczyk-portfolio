## Design Context

### Users
Primary audience: B2B decision-makers looking for a credible AI implementation partner who can connect strategy with execution. Secondary audience: founders looking for a product partner who can move from discovery through MVP to scale. Tertiary audience: recruiters and investors evaluating signal, taste, and delivery track record.

### Brand Personality
Founder-tech, assured, taste-driven. The interface should feel credible, sharp, and premium without becoming cold or corporate. Desired emotional outcome: confidence first, then curiosity and momentum.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark-first experience with a premium technical atmosphere. Think tinted graphite, oxidized teal, restrained brass highlights, and editorial composition layered onto a founder-operator portfolio. Avoid neon AI tropes, gradient text, generic glassmorphism, and anything that reads like a templated startup landing page.

### Design Principles
1. Proof before flourish. Real outcomes and operating style should carry the page.
2. Technical, not sci-fi. The experience should feel engineered and deliberate, not flashy.
3. Founder clarity with editorial pacing. Strong hierarchy, concise statements, and intentional whitespace.
4. Motion as guidance. Animation should direct attention and reinforce confidence, never distract.
5. One premium system. Reuse the same visual language across hero, case studies, capabilities, and contact.
